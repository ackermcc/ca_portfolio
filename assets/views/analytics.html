Asset Performance Management: Analytics

Asset Performance Management is a platform of features and technologies that connects data and analytics with machines and equipment to improve the reliability and availability of industrial assets, minimize costs and reduce operational risks. APM helps minimize surprises, maximize profits and keep people safe. Marketing jargon aside, APM is one of the largest products produced at GE Digital with a variety of features including Asset Ingestion & Management, Alerts & Cases, Dashboards, Analysis, Analytics and with more to come. I was the Lead Interaction Designer for Analytics and a part of the larger APM design team consisting of 10 multi-disciplinary designers.

Analytic Developers and Deployment Engineers need to configure, deploy and manage analytics for industrial assets because properly deploying these analytics provides immense value to analysts and customers.
