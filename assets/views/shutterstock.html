Shutterstock Animation Tutorials

Shutterstock is one of the major stock photography contributors to the world wide web and is well known amongst designers. When I started they had recently IPOed and were striving to be thought leaders in the design community. Adding video content to their platform was an opportunity for them to reach a broader design community.

The design team was tasked with improving their blog by creating small projects and tutorials, especially ones using video related content. At the time, there were only two designers trained in motion design and After Effects, Jordan Roland and myself. I designed and wrote 8 motion design articles including the very first video based tutorial for their blog.

Shutterstock Spectrum is a search tool that allows designers to browse images by color. I worked with a small team to shoot, design and produce the product announcement video.
